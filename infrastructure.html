Infrastructure

Well planned 26000 sq. ft constructed area for plant and machinery and dedicated 25000 sq. ft. constructed area for bag finishing section.

These facilities are augmented with adequate power and water resources.

Also the premises have separate loading and unloading area and in-house weigh scale facility.

Some Statistics About Our

Manufacturing Facility

Extrusion:

2 tape lines having capacity of 450kgs/hrs from LOHIA Corp. Ltd.

Looms

61Nos. – 8 and 6 shuttle looms.

Belt Weaving:

4 needle looms and one machine for filler cord.

Lamination:

150kg per hour Extrusion Coating Plant with a maximum lamination width of 2.2 meters.

Fabric & Belt Cutting:

2 machines for Fabric and Belt Cutting.

Printing:

Four color manual printing machine for jumbo bags and two automatic four color roll to roll printing machine.

Stitching Machine:

75 stitching machines and 2 BCS machines.
